## Overview

Patly uses Gnosis Safe's **Module system** combined with Polymarket's **gasless Builder relayer** to redeem your positions without ever holding your private key.

## The Two Parts

### 1. One-time Setup — Safe Module

Your Polymarket wallet is a [Gnosis Safe](https://safe.global). Gnosis Safe supports **modules** — external addresses that can execute transactions on behalf of the Safe, within strict limits.

When you call `enableModule(patly_wallet)` on your Safe, you're granting Patly's service wallet the ability to call `execTransactionFromModule()` on your Safe. That's it. Patly can only do what you'd explicitly call — in this case, `redeemPositions()` on the Polymarket CTF contract.

```
Your Safe
└── Modules: [0xPatlyWallet]   ← you added this once
```

You can revoke this at any time by calling `disableModule()`.

### 2. Per-Call — Gasless Redeem

When you call `POST /redeem`, Patly:

1. Fetches the `conditionId` for your market slug from Polymarket's API
2. Verifies the market is resolved on-chain (`payoutDenominator > 0`)
3. Encodes `redeemPositions()` calldata targeting the CTF contract
4. Wraps it in `execTransactionFromModule()` — targeting your Safe
5. Submits via Polymarket's **gasless Builder relayer** — no POL needed
6. Your Safe executes the redeem, USDC lands in your wallet

```
POST /redeem
     ↓
Patly encodes redeemPositions() calldata
     ↓
Patly wraps in execTransactionFromModule(your_safe)
     ↓
Polymarket relayer submits gaslessly (Builder keys)
     ↓
Your Safe executes → CTF burns winning tokens → USDC to your wallet
```

## Payment — x402

Each redeem costs **$0.05 USDC**, collected via [x402](https://x402.org) — an open HTTP payment protocol built on EIP-3009.

When you call `POST /redeem` without payment, the server returns **HTTP 402** with payment instructions. The Patly library handles this automatically — it signs a USDC `transferWithAuthorization` and retries the request. No manual steps.

```
POST /redeem  →  402 Payment Required
     ↓
Patly library signs USDC payment (EIP-3009)
     ↓
POST /redeem + X-PAYMENT header  →  200 OK
```

## Security Properties

| Property | Detail |
|---|---|
| **No PK storage** | Patly never stores your private key |
| **Module scope** | Patly can only call `redeemPositions()` — cannot move USDC |
| **Revocable** | Call `disableModule()` on your Safe anytime to revoke |
| **Transparent** | Every tx is visible on Polygonscan |
| **Gasless** | Polymarket's Builder relayer pays gas fees |
